Emmotivity offers really simple royalty free options

Pay once and use the music in almost any type of audio/visual production, as many times as you wish (unlimited needledrops), for as long as you like (in perpetuity).

  • 1

    Standard Use

    Default use. Designed for medium-sized projects. Covers a lot of popular types of use, worldwide. All prices on the website represent the cost of the standard license.

  • 2

    Extended Use

    More advanced use, suitable for big-sized projects. This usage extends the standard usage with many unlimited options and adds wider broadcast use.

  • 3

    Premium Use

    The most advanced use for huge projects. This usage adds almost unlimited use of music in TV projects, major films and commercial theatrical releases worldwide.

  • The usages are upgradeable. You can start with one usages and then upgrade to more advanced usages if necessary. You only pay the difference in price between the usages. Please read our FAQ on how to upgrade.
  • All usages allow you to use the music solely in your own projects. However, if you are working on a client's project, you can purchase a track for and on behalf of your clients.

Prohibited Uses
You may not:
Sell, trade in, give away or distribute the music itself;
Use/include the music in different types of music compilations, such as music compilation CDs/DVDs, music albums, where the tracks are stand-alone elements;
Redistribute the music as a part of different multimedia templates (e.g. website templates, video templates, e-card templates, slideshow templates, etc.) that are subsequently offered to multiple end-users;
Redistribute the music as a part of commercially-produced and commercially-released on-hold systems that are subsequently offered/sold by you to other individuals or companies;
Use or redistribute the music as a part of telephone or mobile phone ringtones;
Create/produce new musical works based on the music (musical compositions, songs) or release and distribute them.
